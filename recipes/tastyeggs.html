Tasty Eggs

Description

Ingredients

  • BBQ Ribeye
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Slice beef into 2-4mm thick slices
  2. Melt butter into pan for beef and eggs
  3. Let steak simmer in the butter for a few minutes
  4. Break eggs into pan and scramble
  5. Add salt to taste while cooking
