Burritos: Another Polish Perspective

Description

This is another recipe that close enough to the real thing. It might not be the best tasting thing available but it's good enough. Authentic burritos require real tortillas that are made from lard, not the garbage that store bought products provide. Making authentic anything means making everything from scratch and somehow finding lard that doesn't have chemicals added to it so good luck with "authentic" anything.

Now cooking is different from baking in that you don't require an actual recipe. But here is a Polish perspective on the matter.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • Sour Cream
  • Pepper Jack Cheese
  • Tortillas
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Add salt and chili powder to beef and cook in pan
  2. Add chopped onion and bell pepper to pan when beef partially cooked
  3. Cook until meat browned and vegetables are softer
  4. Heat tortillas on a pan until warm and pliable
  5. Portion meat and vegetables onto tortillas
  6. Add sour cream and salsa to taste
  7. Fold burrito or learn how fold burrito
